For use on servers with somekind of ent-moveing DLL
such as AVC, Adminop, Entmod, NTF, Flipside etc.
For best results use AVC, most ents can be moved 
with multiples of 128 units
The garage door only seems to work right with AVC 
after you use spawnagain
*NEW* euse a floor or wall ent to change its texture
my AVC script is included, to use it put both AVC files
in you TFC dir and add this line you your autoexec.cfg file
"exec avc.cfg"
you might also need to exec it ingame 
the first time you use it too
the script uses the keypad keys and will overbind them if bound
